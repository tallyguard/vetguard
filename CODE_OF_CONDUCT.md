# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in vetguard a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible
or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and any
conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional
setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project
maintainer through the repository's private contact channels. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
